About AgriScan

Empowering farmers with AI-powered crop disease detection technology

Our Mission

AgriScan aims to revolutionize crop disease management by providing farmers with an accessible, accurate, and offline-capable tool for early disease detection. By combining artificial intelligence with practical agricultural knowledge, we help reduce crop losses, minimize pesticide use, and improve food security worldwide.

The Technology

AgriScan uses a state-of-the-art deep learning model based on MobileNetV2 architecture, specifically optimized for mobile and offline use. Our model has been trained on thousands of images of plant diseases across various crops, enabling it to identify diseases with high accuracy even in challenging field conditions.

Offline Capability

Works without internet connection, perfect for remote fields

Lightweight Model

Optimized for mobile devices with minimal battery consumption

High Accuracy

Over 90% accuracy across 38 different crop diseases

Treatment Recommendations

Provides actionable advice for managing detected diseases

How It Works

  1. 1

    Take a Photo

    Capture an image of the plant leaf showing symptoms using your device's camera or upload an existing image.

  2. 2

    AI Analysis

    Our machine learning model analyzes the image to identify disease patterns and symptoms.

  3. 3

    Get Results

    Receive instant disease identification with confidence score and multiple possibilities.

  4. 4

    Treatment Guidance

    Access specific treatment and prevention recommendations for the identified disease.
